Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

what is the service?

Unify is a platform that allows matchmakers to select candidates for their clients based on social media

can clients use it?

Yes. Clients may access a user account that presents candidates based on their own profile.

How many candidates are in the database?

Thousands, with more being added each day.

Are the candidates national or worldwide?

Both. Our databases presents local candidates, but contains choices from all over the world.

Can I see social media information for a profile when I view it?

Yes. Our platform is fully integrated with Facebook and Linkedin allowing you to make matches based on social and professional backgrounds.

What other features does the platform have?

Unify has comprehensive search ability for matchmakers to find the best choices.

How does the search feature work?

Matchmakers or Clients choose criteria such as education or religion and the feature presents them with multiple candidates.

How much does it cost?

Matchmakers pay to use the service, but their clients can use it free. Please contact our sales office for details.
